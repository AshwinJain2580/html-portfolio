The Best Movies According to Ashwin

My top 3 movies of all-time.

1. Kolar Gold Fields (K.G.F.) - Chapter 1 and 2

This is one of my favourite movie.I really like the these type of gangsters based movies.

2. Kantara

I really like the concept of demigods on which this movie is based upon and the importance of traditional culture namely Kola that is shown in this movie.

3. M.S. Dhoni - The untold story

I really like this motivational movie in which it is shown that how Mahi struggled and made his way into the Indian Cricket Team. I am a big fan of MS Dhoni sir and this movie which is actually his auto biography is really inspiring to me.
